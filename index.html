About

Hello, my name is Nicholas. I'm a Multiverse AMEX apprentice based in Fort Lauderdale.


My sense of curiosity and love of exploration led me to work in the yachting industry, where I had the chance to travel to some amazing places, from the Caribbean to Europe and the United States. In that role, I learned how to work effectively in a team and how to think on my feet under pressure, skills that I've found to be valuable in all areas of life.


However, I knew that I was ready for a new challenge, so I decided to dive into the world of coding. As soon as I started learning, I knew that I had found something that I loved. Web development in particular captured my imagination, and I was eager to learn as much as I could about this exciting field.


That's why I enrolled in the Software Engineer Immersive course at General Assembly. This program has allowed me to hone my technical skills and explore my creative interests in a supportive and challenging environment.

Now, I'm part of the amazing team at AMEX where I am actively engaging in expanding my knowledge of computer programming by diving into Java and collaborating with a group of very talented individuals.
